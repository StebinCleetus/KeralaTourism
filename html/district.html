Explore Kerala

KERALA has its own heritage and culture. Kerala is the most attractive tourist destination with its beaches, mountain resorts, backwaters, birds and sanctuaries of wild life, etc. Kerala is blessed with its variety of flora and fauna. Many tourists come to Kerala festivals. Kerala is the first fully literate country in India. Kerala cinema is still a symbol of quality films. In the literary domain also Kerala is blessed with so many legends.
